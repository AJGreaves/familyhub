About

Family Hub was created by Anna Greaves, a British mother of two, who moved to The Netherlands in 2003. Born from the need to get out and connected to the community when moving into a new place, the Family Hub goal is to inform and connect english speaking families to the events and activities available in the local area.

Whether you're here for a visit, or here to stay, there's lots of fun activities and events for all ages and interests!
